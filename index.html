The Hunter x Hunter manga is back!
New chapters get release date

Shueisha has officially announced that new chapters will be released starting October 7, 2024.

The news of new chapters coincides with the release of volume 38 on September 4, 2024, which compiled previously serialized chapters into a single volume for the first time.

Shueisha confirmed that Hunter x Hunter will not return with a weekly release schedule.

Instead, the series will follow a more flexible timeline, differing from its previous runs. During the last return from hiatus, chapters 391 to 400 were released before the series paused again.

Volume 38, which consists of chapters 391 to 400, continues the intense Succession Contest arc. This perilous storyline centers around the fourteen princes of the Kakin Empire, who are forced into a deadly battle for the throne as their ship, the Black Whale, sets sail for the mysterious Dark Continent.
